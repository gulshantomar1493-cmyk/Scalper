MarketScalper — Help Center / Madad

🎯 Getting Started

MarketScalper ek market ANALYSIS tool hai — ye khud trade NAHI karta. Ye tumhe batata hai ki kab, kahan aur kyun ek setup ban raha hai. Order tum khud apne exchange par lagate ho.

Shuru kaise karein: Left sidebar se pages badlo — Live (main screen), Replay, Trade Review, Journal, Analytics, Settings. Live screen par: beech mein candle chart, upar coin + timeframe, right side mein analysis panel.

Naye ho? Sidebar mein Beginner Mode ON rakho — har control ke neeche simple helper text dikhega.

📡 Scanner

Upar green "Scanner running" pill dikhta hai — matlab tool continuously BTC aur ETH ko scan kar raha hai, har minute.

Status badalta rehta hai (jaise "Checking liquidity", "Searching trade setups") — ye bata raha hai abhi kya check ho raha hai. "last scan HH:MM" = aakhri scan ka time. Green dot = live connected.

📈 Chart

  • Green candle = price upar gaya, red = neeche.
  • Upar coin buttons (BTCUSDT / ETHUSDT) — click karke coin badlo; chart apne aap naye coin ke price par fit ho jata hai.
  • Timeframe (1m … 1M): 1m/5m par analysis milta hai; bade TF sirf context ke liye.
  • Mouse se zoom/pan karo. Reset = default view wapas. Auto = price auto-fit on/off.
  • Neeche-daayein countdown = current candle band hone mein kitna time.
  • Draw menu se trendline / horizontal line / rectangle / fibonacci bana sakte ho.

🎯 Trade Setup

Right side ka Recommendation card = current trade setup.

  • LONG (buy) ya SHORT (sell) + grade (A+ best, A, B) + confidence % + stars.
  • Verdict: TRADEABLE = achha setup; BELOW THRESHOLD = abhi kamzor.
  • "Why?" par click karke saari safety checks (gates) + reasons dekho.
  • Trade Plan card: Entry, Stop-loss, Target 1 & 2, Risk:Reward — ye sirf SUGGESTION hai, order tum khud lagao.

Yaad rakho: setup sirf 1m/5m par milta hai.

📊 Indicators

Chart par EMA (moving average lines), SMA, RSI aur Volume laga sakte ho. Upar "Indicators ▾" menu se on/off karo aur period (jaise EMA 20/50/200) change karo.

Ye sab backend mein compute hote hain — har device par same value.

🏗️ Market Structure

Market kaise move kar raha hai: HH (higher high), HL (higher low), LH (lower high), LL (lower low).

HH + HL = Uptrend (upar). LH + LL = Downtrend (neeche). Right side ka Market Structure box live events stream karta hai (swings, sweeps, BOS, CHOCH).

⛓️ BOS (Break of Structure)

BOS = trend continue hone ka signal. Uptrend mein price last high ke upar close kare = BOS UP (trend aage badh raha). Downtrend mein last low ke neeche = BOS DOWN.

🔄 CHOCH (Change of Character)

CHOCH = trend palatne (reverse) ka pehla signal. Uptrend mein price last low ke neeche toot jaye = CHOCH DOWN (uptrend kamzor). Ye ek warning hai — abhi confirm nahi; structure ka wait karo.

💧 Liquidity

Liquidity = jahan bahut saare stop-loss orders pade hain — equal highs (EQH) / equal lows (EQL).

Sweep = price un levels ko touch karke wapas palat jaana (stops hunt karke). Sweep + CHOCH = strong reversal setup.

🔭 HTF Signals

Right panel mein "Higher-Timeframe Bias" — 15m/1h/4h/1d ka overall direction (BULLISH / BEARISH / NEUTRAL) + score + confidence. Har TF ka card: trend, structure, momentum.

"✓ aligned / ✗ conflicting with your signal" = HTF tumhare 1m/5m signal se agree karta hai ya nahi. Ye sirf context hai — execution 1m/5m par hi hota hai.

📓 Journal

Apne trades, notes aur lessons log karo — kya socha, entry/exit/SL/TP, kya galti hui, kya seekha, emotion, tags.

Purane trades review karke apni mistakes se seekho — ek acha trader apna journal maintain karta hai.

⚙️ Settings

  • Theme (dark / light) aur Beginner Mode on/off.
  • Notifications: Telegram + desktop alerts — naya setup aane par ping.
  • Symbols / timeframes / API host ki info.

❓ FAQ

Q: Ye tool khud trade karta hai?
Nahi. Sirf analysis; order tum lagao.

Q: Signal kyun nahi mil raha?
Ya to safety gates pass nahi hue, ya score threshold se kam hai.

Q: Strategy TRUSTED kab hoti hai?
200+ recommendations par, fees ke baad positive result aane par. Tab tak signals blindly follow mat karo.

Q: Real paise lagte hain?
Nahi — ye decision-support tool hai, koi real order nahi.

Q: Bade timeframe par signal kyun nahi?
Execution sirf 1m/5m par; bade TF sirf context ke liye.
