Ye guide batati hai ki engine chart par kya-kya draw karta hai, wo kyun important hai, aur engine + traders usko kaise use karte hain. Sab simple Hinglish mein. Yaad rahe: ye ANALYSIS tool hai — trade tum khud manually loge.

Swing High / Swing Low

Kya hai? Chart par local upar ka point (swing high) aur niche ka point (swing low) — jahan se price ne turn liya.

Kyun important? Market ki poori structure inhi points se banti hai — HH/HL (upar) ya LH/LL (niche).

Engine kaise use karta hai? Har timeframe par swings detect karke trend, BOS, CHOCH, trendlines aur zones banata hai.

Traders kaise use karte hain? Swing ke upar/niche stop-loss rakhte hain; break hone par trend change samajhte hain.

Market Structure (HH / HL / LH / LL)

Kya hai? Ye batata hai market upar chal raha hai ya niche. Higher-High + Higher-Low = uptrend; Lower-High + Lower-Low = downtrend.

Kyun important? Trend ke saath trade karna sabse high-probability hota hai.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Har TF ki structure se bias ladder banata hai (1D sabse zyada weight). Reversal sirf ladder ke SAATH deta hai, against nahi.

Traders kaise use karte hain? "Trend is your friend" — structure dekh ke direction choose karte hain.

BOS — Break of Structure

Kya hai? Price ne trend ki direction mein pichhla swing TOD diya — matlab trend continue.

Kyun important? Confirm karta hai ki current trend zinda hai.

Engine kaise use karta hai? BOS se trend confirm hota hai; displacement wale BOS ke pichhe Order Block banata hai.

Traders kaise use karte hain? BOS ke baad pullback par entry dhundhte hain.

CHOCH — Change of Character

Kya hai? Price ne PEHLI baar opposite side ka swing tod diya — trend badalne ka pehla signal.

Kyun important? Reversal ka sabse reliable structural clue.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Zone par CHOCH = strongest confirmation; counter-trend setup CHOCH ke bina issue nahi hota.

Traders kaise use karte hain? CHOCH ke baad naye trend ki taraf position banate hain.

Liquidity (stops ka pool)

Kya hai? Jahan bahut saare traders ke stop-loss pade hote hain — highs ke upar (buy-side) aur lows ke niche (sell-side).

Kyun important? Price aksar inhi levels ki taraf khichti hai (draw), kyunki wahan orders bhare hue hain.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Har pool ko rank karta hai (PDH/PDL, weekly = ★5) aur TP targets inhi par rakhta hai. Sweep hone par reversal ka "fuel" maanta hai.

Traders kaise use karte hain? Liquidity ke paas profit book karte hain, ya sweep ke baad reversal khelte hain.

Equal High / Equal Low (EQH / EQL)

Kya hai? Do ya zyada highs (ya lows) bilkul same level par — double/triple top jaisa.

Kyun important? Wahan stops ka bada cluster hota hai — magnet ki tarah.

Engine kaise use karta hai? EQH/EQL ko ★4 liquidity pool banata hai; sweep ke baad track karta hai (reverse hua ya continue).

Traders kaise use karte hain? Equal highs ke upar ka sweep aksar short ka mauka deta hai (aur ulta).

Fresh / Tested / Broken Zone

Kya hai? Har zone ki life hoti hai: FRESH = abhi tak touch nahi hua (sabse strong) → TESTED = ek baar react ho chuka → WEAK = 3+ baar touch (orders khatam) → BROKEN = price ne close ke saath tod diya → RETIRED.

Kyun important? Fresh zone par reaction ka chance sabse zyada; baar-baar test hua zone tootne wala hota hai.

Engine kaise use karta hai? FRESH component ko confluence maanta hai; BROKEN demand role-flip hoke supply ban jaata hai (aur ulta).

Traders kaise use karte hain? Fresh zone ka pehla retest = classic entry.

Order Block (OB)

Kya hai? Bade move se pehle ki aakhri opposite candle — jahan institutions ne orders bhare the.

Kyun important? Price wapas aake aksar wahin se react karti hai.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Displacement BOS ke pichhe OB zone banata hai; entry-zones mein count hota hai.

Traders kaise use karte hain? OB tak pullback ka wait karke enter karte hain.

FVG — Fair Value Gap

Kya hai? 3 candles ke beech ka price gap — market itni tez bhagi ki beech mein trade hi nahi hua.

Kyun important? Price aksar is gap ko "fill" karne wapas aati hai.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Bade FVG (≥0.3×ATR) ko zone banata hai, fill hone tak track karta hai.

Traders kaise use karte hain? FVG ke 50% par entry lagate hain.

Trendline

Kya hai? 2+ swings ko jodne wali line — rising support ya falling resistance.

Kyun important? Jitne zyada touches, utni important line — uske aas-paas orders cluster hote hain.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Har TF par lines lifecycle ke saath banata hai (NEW→VALID→STRONG→WEAK→BROKEN); sirf VALID/STRONG lines zones aur confluence mein count hoti hain.

Traders kaise use karte hain? Line par bounce, ya line break ke baad retest trade karte hain.

Premium / Discount

Kya hai? Range ke upar wala half = premium (mehnga), niche wala half = discount (sasta).

Kyun important? Sasta kharido, mehnga becho — discount mein LONG, premium mein SHORT.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Reversal LONG sirf discount mein, SHORT sirf premium mein allow karta hai.

Traders kaise use karte hain? Range ka 50% nikaal ke sirf sahi half mein trade karte hain.

Breakout / Breakdown

Kya hai? Price ne resistance ke UPAR (breakout) ya support ke NICHE (breakdown) displacement ke saath close kiya, phir wapas aake level ko HOLD kiya (retest).

Kyun important? Level ka haath badal gaya — ab wahi level ulta kaam karega. Trend sessions (London/NY) mein ye best pattern hai.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Displaced break + retest-hold ke baad hi setup deta hai; entry = broken level, SL = level ke andar wapas.

Traders kaise use karte hain? Break ke baad retest par enter karte hain — chase nahi karte.

Reversal (Zone Reversal)

Kya hai? Price ek strong zone par aayi, wahan se REJECT hui (rejection wick / engulfing / CHOCH), aur wapas mud gayi.

Kyun important? Zone + confirmation + liquidity sweep = high-probability turn.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Sirf mapped zone par, HTF ladder ke SAATH, sahi premium/discount half mein, 5m confirmation ke baad — tab setup issue hota hai.

Traders kaise use karte hain? Sweep ke baad ki rejection candle par entry, wick ke pichhe stop.

Session Timing (IST)

Kya hai? Din ke alag time par market ka behaviour alag hota hai — London+NY overlap (19:30–22:30 IST) sabse best; raat 02:00–05:30 dead zone.

Engine kaise use karta hai? Dead windows mein setups BLOCK, lunch chop (11:30–13:30) mein grade downgrade, best windows mein breakout ko boost.

Grade (A+ / A) aur Confluence

Kya hai? Grade = kitne independent reasons ek saath agree kar rahe (7 mein se). A+ = 5+, A = 3+. Ye probability % NAHI hai — sirf agreement ka count.

Kyun important? Jitne zyada reasons, utna strong setup. Engine kabhi fake confidence % nahi dikhata.
