# DECISION P3.12–P3.16 — Strategy Engine detail set (D20, §5 pins)

**Date:** 2026-07-18 · **Status:** Accepted (autonomous execution
directive; pins per the D9–D19 precedent — §5 transcribed, open details
resolved via established conventions, A15 folded inline as the roadmap
mandates) · **Scope:** implementation decision only — zero architecture
change. Sole specification for the Strategy Engine (roadmap P3.12–P3.16).

## D20.1 — Template (P3.12) and outputs

One `StrategyEngine` per symbol evaluates the three §5 strategies per
closed 1m candle over frozen-engine outputs only (consumed, never
recomputed). Output: immutable `Signal` records — `strategy` (S1/S2/S3),
`direction` (LONG/SHORT), `entry`, `sl`, `tp1`, `tp2` (None allowed),
`created_ts`, `facts` (deterministic rule-trace strings, §8),
`invalid_after_bars` (the strategy's INVALID window, enforced by P4.2).
The CONTEXT→SETUP→CONFIRM→ENTRY/SL/TP/INVALID template is realized as
per-strategy pure fold state; a strategy emits at most one signal per
bar; multiple strategies may emit on the same bar (deterministic order
S1, S2, S3). All price/ATR inputs are the same-bar frozen values.
Composition feeds signals to the D17 planner (its recorded first
consumer); planner-rejected plans emit no recommendation (P3.18).

## D20.2 — S1 Liquidity Sweep Reversal

- SETUP+CONFIRM(a): the frozen `SweepShift` event IS "sweep then CHOCH
  within 3×1m" (D12.5) — S1 arms directly on SweepShift. Direction:
  sweep side HIGH → SHORT, LOW → LONG. Reversal check (pinned after the
  V2 probe): D12.5 pairing is direction-blind by frozen design, so the
  strategy itself verifies the paired CHOCH broke in the trade
  direction — LOW sweep requires CHOCH UP, HIGH sweep CHOCH DOWN. The
  engine keeps a ts-addressable record of recent ChochEvent directions
  (choch_ts lookup; window ≥ the D12.5 3-bar tag lag); an unknown or
  opposing CHOCH direction → no signal (D7). A sweep whose paired CHOCH
  continues the break (the P2.24 gate-episode shape: LOW sweep → CHOCH
  DOWN) is a breakdown story, never an S1 reversal.
- CONTEXT: 5m alignment — either the 5m trend (a composition-owned 5m
  `TrendState` instance fed by the existing labeled 5m pivots; the D10
  machine is tf-generic) matches the trade direction, OR the A8
  premium/discount extreme aligns (HIGH sweep in premium, LOW sweep in
  discount). Both None/misaligned → no signal (D7).
- CONFIRM(b): a D15 confluence zone with `count ≥ 2`, direction matching
  the trade (LONG→BULL), within 0.3×ATR of the CHOCH bar's close (the
  D16.3 proximity precedent). First qualifying zone in the D15 order is
  the entry zone.
- ENTRY: the entry zone's midpoint ((lo+hi)/2 — the §5 "OB/FVG 50%
  level"). SL: beyond the sweep candle's wick ± 0.25×ATR (LONG: sweep
  low − buffer). TP1: nearest opposing pool (LONG: cheapest EQH pool
  price > entry); required ≥ entry ± 1R (§5 "1R minimum") else no
  signal; no pool → no signal (D7). TP2: the A8 external swing (LONG:
  external 5m H price, fed to the engine by composition alongside the
  liquidity feed); absent or not beyond TP1 → TP2 = None.
- INVALID: `invalid_after_bars = 5` (§5: entry unfilled in 5 candles).

## D20.3 — S2 Trend Pullback Continuation (A15 folded)

- A15 (verbatim roadmap resolution): impulse = the BOS leg — last
  confirmed opposite swing price → the BOS-breaking close, on 1m.
  Tracked from the frozen BosEvent (`broken_pivot` is the leg's far end:
  the leg spans the last opposite-side confirmed pivot before the BOS →
  pinned: impulse_low = the most recent confirmed L pivot price before
  the BOS bar (LONG case), impulse_high = the BOS close).
- CONTEXT: composition 5m TrendState matches direction AND close vs
  session VWAP aligns (LONG: c > vwap). VWAP None → no setup (D7).
- SETUP: after a with-trend 1m BOS, a pullback whose extreme enters ANY
  of: an unmitigated OB zone, an unfilled FVG zone, the session VWAP
  (candle range crosses it), or a trendline TOUCH event bar; AND the
  retrace depth (impulse_high − pullback_low)/(impulse_high −
  impulse_low) ∈ [0.30, 0.70] inclusive (LONG; mirror SHORT); AND rvol
  at the setup bar < rvol at the BOS bar (declining volume; either
  None → fails, D7).
- CONFIRM: a later close back in trend direction with body > 0.8×ATR
  strict AND rvol ≥ 1.2 inclusive (None fails). Entry = confirm close.
- SL: pullback extreme ∓ 0.25×ATR. TP1: impulse_high (LONG; ≥1R
  required else no signal). TP2: pullback_low + 1.618 × (impulse_high −
  impulse_low) (LONG; §5's 1.618 extension; mirror SHORT); not beyond
  TP1 → None. INVALID: 5 bars (template default; §5 names none for S2).
- Setup window: a pullback that breaks the impulse start (full
  retrace > 100%) or a new opposite BOS cancels the setup.

## D20.4 — S3 Trendline Fake-Break Trap

- SETUP+CONFIRM(a): the frozen `FAKE_BREAK` TrendlineEvent (validated
  ≥3-touch line, re-entry close — the event fires ON the re-entry bar
  per D11.9). Direction: support fake-break → LONG, resistance → SHORT.
- CONFIRM(b): rvol ≥ 1.5 inclusive (None fails, D7) AND no opposing
  key level or pool strictly between entry and entry ± 1R (§5 "no
  opposing HTF level within 1R"), where R = |entry − SL|.
- ENTRY: the re-entry (event) candle's close. SL: the fake-break
  extreme ∓ 0.25×ATR, extreme = the excursion min/max of the last
  `FAKE_BREAK_WINDOW + 1` candles up to the event bar (the watch span;
  the event does not carry the extreme, the engine tracks a 4-bar
  rolling window of raw candles). TP1: the last confirmed opposite-side
  1m pivot price (§5 "/ last swing"; ≥1R required else no signal).
  TP2: None. INVALID: 5 bars.

## D20.5 — Inputs, cadence, composition, payload

Inputs per bar (all frozen outputs, composition-fed): candle; ATR
value; this bar's BosEvent/ChochEvent/TrendlineEvents/
SweepEvents/SweepShifts; confluence zones (uncapped); OB blocks; FVG
gaps; pools; key levels; premium_discount; session_vwap; rvol; 5m trend
state (new composition-owned `TrendState` instance — engine reuse, zero
frozen-code change); external 5m pivot prices (H and L, fed via
`on_external_pivot` alongside the liquidity feed); confirmed 1m pivots
(via `on_pivot`, for S3 TP and S2 impulse legs). The 1m trend state is
NOT an input (freeze-audit amendment): no strategy clause consumes it —
S2's 1m alignment is already guaranteed by the with-trend arming BOS,
and requiring it to persist through the pullback would suppress the
§5-typical setup (the D10 band legitimately reads RANGE during pullback
consolidation). Cadence: strategy
evaluation runs AFTER qualification in `step()` (last consumer), once
per closed candle (the D19.8 lock discipline). Payload gains additive
key `"signals"`: the last 10 emitted signals per symbol (deque,
`_EVENTS_SHOWN` precedent). Persistence of signals is P3.18's — this
engine emits objects only (R1 discipline).

## D20.6 — Determinism and D7

Pure fold; no clock, no randomness. Every unavailable input (unwarm
rvol/ATR/VWAP, missing pools/zones/levels, None trend) fails the
requiring condition — signals are never emitted on incomplete evidence
(D7). Unseeded runs (no 20-day RVOL history) therefore never fire
S2/S3 (their confirms require rvol) — recorded, expected; S1 carries no
rvol requirement and fires unseeded.

## Addenda — freeze-audit pins and accepted readings (2026-07-19)

Recorded per the D14–D19 convention after the three-agent freeze audit
(conformance / adversarial / test-claims). Code changes made at audit:
the S2 setup's extra 1m-trend conjunct was REMOVED (conformance
blocker: it suppressed the §5-typical pullback whenever the memoryless
D10 band read RANGE mid-consolidation; §5's CONTEXT heading is
"(5m)" and the arming BOS already guarantees 1m alignment — D20.5
amended accordingly); the S2 depth fact now cites the SETUP-bar depth
(frozen into the impulse when the rule passes) so the §8 trace can
never contradict the [0.30, 0.70] rule it reports, while SL/TP2 keep
using the live pullback extreme per the D20.3 letter;
`S3_EXTREME_WINDOW` is derived as `trendline.FAKE_BREAK_WINDOW + 1`
(single source of truth).

Pinned readings (behavior kept, now normative):

1. **S2 OB/FVG zone-touch is direction-matched** (BULL zones for LONG,
   BEAR for SHORT) even though §5's union is undirectioned — a supply
   zone is not pullback-support for a LONG. The trendline TOUCH arm
   stays side-blind (the spec letter; a validated line under the
   pullback is support regardless of its label). Asymmetry deliberate.
2. **Zone-touch = candle-range overlap** with the zone interval
   (inclusive, the D13 mitigation precedent), not a running-extreme
   point test; the VWAP arm is the clause's own "candle range crosses
   it" wording.
3. **A confirm that passes body+rvol consumes the impulse** even when
   the 1R minimum then rejects the signal (confirm consummates the
   CONTEXT→SETUP→CONFIRM sequence; one confirm per impulse). A confirm
   attempt that fails body or rvol does NOT consume — the setup keeps
   waiting. Cancellation set stays exactly {full retrace close,
   opposite BOS} + re-arm-replacement.
4. **Cancellation is close-based** (project-uniform close-through
   convention): a wick beyond the impulse start with a close back
   inside does not cancel; the wick still deepens the tracked extreme,
   so SL and the 1.618 TP2 use it, and a wick past 70% before setup
   blocks the setup via the depth rule.
5. **Degenerate geometry guards** (`r <= 0` → no signal) in all three
   strategies are unpinned by §5's text but conservative: the D17
   planner's geometry validation would reject the same plans, so
   recommendation-level behavior is identical.
6. **One-bar blind spot** (adversarial finding, accepted): on a bar
   carrying a same-direction BOS whose leg check fails (requires an L
   pivot confirmed at/above the BOS close — a narrow relabeling
   window), the surviving impulse skips that bar's cancellation and
   extreme tracking; deterministic, bounded, self-corrects next bar.
7. **`_choch_dirs` window**: in the frozen liquidity engine
   `SweepShift.choch_ts == SweepShift.ts` always (pairing happens on
   the CHOCH's own bar), so the ts-lookup always hits the same-bar
   entry; the 8-deep deque is defensive margin over the D12.5 wording,
   not load-bearing.
8. **S1's "CHOCH bar's close" = the shift bar's close** is exact, not
   approximate, for the same reason as (7).
